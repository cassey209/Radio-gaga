OUR PHILOSOPHY

My story begins in Hanoi, Vietnam. As a nomad, I am naturally attracted to people who love to wander as well, and together with our trip, they would take me overseas whenever possible â€“ ultimately helping to inspire my love of multiculturalism, design, and architecture.

Through experiencing different cultures, seeing rich and poor people, happy and unhappy people, I started to wonder who I was, and asked myself what kind of life I wanted to live?

I reflected on them over and over. Eventually, I found my answers. I want to live a simple and down-to-earth life. I only need the basics to be satisfied: food, clothing and a house. And my last answer was coffee and my country. This is why I founded Radio Gaga.
