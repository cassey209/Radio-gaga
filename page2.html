Our Farm

The best coffee comes from the best place to grow Arabica coffeee in Viet Nam

Have you ever wondered what stages the aromatic cup of coffee in your hands experienced? When the waitress brings you a cup of coffee, it is just the end of a long and wonderful journey: from choosing the right coffee bean, screening the seeds, planting trees, tending and collecting.

There are literally thousands of choices when it comes to choosing good coffee beans. If you choose the wrong ones, you won't be brewing amazing coffee. There is no #1 best bean because a great choice for one brewing method is a terrible choice for another, howerver, the best coffee comes from people who care.  

From our first shops, we have started working with Cau Dat Farm to grow coffee according to our own standards. The dewy land at an altitude of 1,650m above sea level is the best place to grow Arabica coffee in Vietnam. It is the "golden height" that gives Arabica coffee beans fragrant light and excellent sour.

We committed to helping farming communities endure and thrive. To extend our reach and impact, we work with organizations that have expertise in these agricultural communities.
