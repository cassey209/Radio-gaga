Key Signature Drinks

Sapa sour

$5

Blended with the perfect ratio of Espresso double, whipping cream and condensed milk. Sapa (name of the best destination must-have experiences in Vietnam) is also a must-try drink when coming to Radio gaga by holding the number one position in our list of key signatures.

Saigon taste

$5

From the premium arabica in Da Lat, we have created an aromatic coffee combined with yogurt. Saigon is thick, bringing a pleasant mild sour taste when getting the first sip then a persistent sweet aftertaste in the mouth can be the point of this cup. Because of an unique taste, you will never find another drink like Saigon anywhere else in the world. It will release its flavours when served with ice. In hot summer days, Saigon does not disappoint, by bringing a fresh taste, surprisingly interesting.

Cloudy walk

$5

It takes us a very long time to find out a cup of drink that enchants every taste buds. This tough challenge inspired me to make all the best flavors for Cloudy walk. Cloudy walk is simply made from a double shot of Espresso blended with buttery bobbing whipping cream and a bit of condensed milk.
